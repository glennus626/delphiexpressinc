About Us

With a combined 27 years of freight management experience, our team of brokerage professionals will work hard to make sure your freight is where it needs to be, when it needs to be there.

 

Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver quality transportation services at fair and affordable rates. Unlike many transportation companies today, we maintain pride in what we do. As a result, our company policies are not derived merely from the "minimum" requirements set forth by law but also the laws of "common courtesy" and the "Golden Rule". That is not to say that there will never be a problem. Anything can happen. Although it does mean that if there is a problem, we'll treat your freight as if it was ours.
